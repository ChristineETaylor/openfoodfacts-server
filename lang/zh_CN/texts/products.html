Open Food Facts 上产品数量的演变 ——

Open Food Facts 公开发布于 2012 年 5 月 19 日（这个网站在 2012 年初为 beta 测试人员开放）。 该数据库包含 648 个产品。

This graph shows the evolution of the number of food products listed in the Open Food Facts database and the number of products with complete information (photos and data).

You can help us by adding products (with the iPhone, Android and Windows Phone apps), but also by completing the product pages to be completed using the photos sent by other contributors.
